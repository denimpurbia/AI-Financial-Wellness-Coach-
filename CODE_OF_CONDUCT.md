# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone who participates in the AI Financial Wellness Coach community will experience harassment-free collaboration, irrespective of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Sex characteristics
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Education
- Socio-economic status
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

✅ **Positive Interactions**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Acknowledging mistakes and learning from them

❌ **Unacceptable Behavior**
- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Violence or threats of violence
- Discriminatory jokes and language

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If someone is behaving unacceptably, the community as a whole is empowered to either take informal action or report directly to the project maintainers.

**Report to**: conduct@example.com

Please include:
- Your contact information
- Names of those involved
- Description of the incident
- Context where possible

All reports will be kept confidential.

### Response to Reports

Community leaders are responsible for:
1. Clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior
2. Taking appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior
3. Removing, editing, or rejecting comments, code, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
4. Temporarily or permanently banning contributors whose behavior is harmful

### Consequences

1. **Warning**: First-time violations
2. **Temporary Ban**: Repeated violations (24 hours to 30 days)
3. **Permanent Ban**: Serious violations or repeated behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within project spaces (GitHub, Discord, forums, etc.)
- When representing the project in public spaces
- When advocating for the project
- When using the project to conduct business

This Code of Conduct does not apply to:
- Conduct occurring outside of project spaces
- Conduct that doesn't relate to the project or community
